Sociality

Social stats for the internet


# What should sociality be?

* A complete social network stats gatherer
* A way to easily share stats about sites and URLs
* A dashboard for your sites social media interactions

## What can it do?

* Sociality score widget for your page (configure what social networks you want)
* Intensive comparison between various sites, eg: news.com.au vs smh.com.au, etc...
* Create dashboards and reports on social stats

## How can you monetize it?

* A paid account will allow you to claim your site, and adjust:
	* how often it is crawled
	* Various drill-down capabilities
* The paid accounts will have various tiers, also allowing:
	* Ability to see dashboards that show how well your whole site is doing
	* Ability to group (tag) stories
	* Ability to see detailed aggregate data
	* Choose what URLs are included in your own reports by applying filtering to a dashboard

## How does it do it?

* Add a URL and it will be crawled every X hours, using a node.js based scraper.
* By default it will crawl 1 links deep  (Admin can of course configure this).
